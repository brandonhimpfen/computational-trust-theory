# Terminology

## Claim
A proposition that may be true or false.

## Evidence
Information that supports or contradicts one or more claims.

## Source
The origin of evidence, such as a document, dataset, sensor, or system.

## Provenance
The recorded history describing how a claim or evidence object was created, transformed, or derived.

## Trust
A quantitative or qualitative assessment representing justified confidence in a claim.

## Confidence
A system's reported estimate regarding the correctness of its output.

## Uncertainty
The degree to which available information is incomplete or ambiguous.

## Contradiction
Evidence that reduces confidence in one or more claims by supporting incompatible propositions.

## Trust Graph
A graph whose nodes represent claims, evidence, sources, and computational activities, while edges describe relationships among them.
